ABOUT US
As a creative, you are constantly in pursuit of finding an authentic and real voice. It’s understandable that you want it to be able to speak for itself. You don’t want to explain to someone why they should buy your art.

Learning how to write & speak about art in a way that allows potential customers to recognize its value helps potential clients realize why they need your art in their lives.

Mantraa Luminosity is a high concept design, marketing & distribution company delivering fresh, memorable yet effective creative design & marketing campaigns for over 500 Feature films for established and new age film makers. We’re telling stories, powerfully.

VALUE

Our values are both our identity and our promise defining and motivating our every interaction with you. We strive for that every day because we want to be a company we are proud of. Our core 6 values are…

OUR TEAM

Mantraa Luminosity has the finest creative minds led by Chaitanya Sant whose exemplary creative work over 3 decades has set a benchmark in creative standards with our dynamic young team. The team also boasts of Ranjan Singh who has an enviable track record of delivering creative and unique visual promotion campaigns. The third musketeer is Rahul Merchant who has over 200 film marketing campaigns to his credit.

CHAITANYA SANT

Managing Partner & Creative Lead

A graduate from Sr J.J School of Arts, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India Chaitanya has been a leading contributor in the space of publicity design, specially Hindi feature films such as Padmaavat, Bajirao Mastani, Rowdy Rathore, Mary Kom, Life in a Metro, Khosla Ka Ghosla, Bluffmaster, Taxi no 9211 and more for more than 25 years. Chaitanya is a perfect combination of Art & storytelling. To tell a story with a single image , itself is a task, which Chaitanya has mastered. The Same Perception will be echoed by leading Directors & producers from Indian Film Fraternity. After working for feature films as a publicity designer he is now venturing into content as a producer since 2020.

Chaitanya also holds the unique place as Director of India’s first 3D Ad film produced for Pidilite’s ACRON RANGEELA, a brand sponsor on kids film “CHHOTA JADUGAR 3D”.

RAHUL MERCHANT

Managing Partner & Director Business

21 years of a vast & varied experience across media and entertainment marketing, including television, feature films & new media with a career kick off in Television @ Star TV in Channel [V] followed by Channel 9 of Nine Network, Australia handling large-format ground & TV events & integrated marketing initiatives for 5 years. Transitioned to into Motion Pictures Marketing with Group Shringar (Shringar Films & Fame Cinemas), Group Percept to collectively market of over 100+ feature films.

Plunged into Mobile VAS, as India head, for Bubble Motion Pvt. Ltd., (A Singtel Funded company) which included strategizing for customer engagement & content delivery with India’s very first voice-blogging product called “Bubbly” featuring A+ Indian Film & Sports Stars.

An entrepreneur of 12 years+, founded Luminosity Pictures in 2011, skilled in theatrical film marketing, distribution & content syndication across Satellite & OTT Platforms.

Founding Partner of CR8 Branded Content Solutions doing stellar work with Brands, Celebrities and influencers.

Continue to be a visiting faculty at MICA, Ahmedabad taking the “Film Marketing” course for the New Media senior students, since 2017...

RANJAN SINGH

CREATIVE CONSULTANT & LEAD: Video Division.

Ranjan Singh is a Film/Media professional with 20+ years of industry experience, comprising of producing, distributing and marketing films/content.
